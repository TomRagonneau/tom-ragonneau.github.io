About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Applied Mathematics of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Dr. Zaikun Zhang and Prof. Xiaojun Chen.

I am broadly interested in mathematical optimization and its applications, mainly on

  • methods based on inaccurate information,
  • derivative-free methods.

I am currently working on PDFO, a cross-platform package providing MATLAB and Python interfaces for using late Professor M. J. D. Powell's derivative-free optimization solvers.
