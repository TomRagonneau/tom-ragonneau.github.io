About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Applied Mathematics at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Dr. Zaikun Zhang and Prof. Xiaojun Chen.

I am broadly interested in mathematical optimization and its applications, mainly on methods based on inaccurate information and methods dedicated to derivative-free optimization.
